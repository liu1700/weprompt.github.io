Google API Services Disclosure

WordForge use Google's API

WordForge uses Google's Application Programming Interface (API) Services to enable the user authorization feature for accessing the WordForge platform. (i.e. use of your Gmail account to login to WordForge platform)

WordForge's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use Requirements.

Your Google information is used by our system developers to provide or improve user-facing features that are prominent to your user experience. WordForge policies and procedures define requirements that prohibit the unauthorized use of data within the platform.

We place significant importance on maintaining the security and privacy of your data. Reference our Privacy Policy for information on our privacy policies regarding the use of the WePrompt platform and its services.
